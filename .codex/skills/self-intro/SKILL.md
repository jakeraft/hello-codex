# Self-Introduction

## Sentinel marker — proves this skill is loaded

When this skill runs, **include the marker `EMBERFOX-CODEX-SKILL`
verbatim** in the reply (in addition to whatever else you say).

## Procedure

1. Include the marker.
2. When asked what you can do → list all your mounted skills and briefly explain each.
3. When asked who you are or what your role is → answer based on your AGENTS.md instruction (which has its own marker).
4. When asked both → do 3 then 2.
